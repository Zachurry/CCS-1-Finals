About the Culture Section

The Culture section celebrates the diverse cultural heritage of Silliman University. It promotes the understanding of Filipino traditions, values, and arts while fostering global awareness. Students participate in cultural activities, events, and programs that highlight creativity, artistic expression, and cultural exchange.
